Frequently Asked

How do I reserve a bed?

Click any available room, choose a bed, fill out the short reservation form, and submit. You'll receive an email confirmation once your reservation is recorded.

Is a deposit required?

Reservations are held pending confirmation. Deposit details are shared via email after admin review.

What's included in the rent?

Bed, study desk, chair, and cooling. Utilities and Wi-Fi policies are communicated during check-in.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes. Contact the administration and your reservation will be released.
